🛒 STS → Rio Grande Cart Bookmarklet

Install

Drag the red button below to your browser's bookmarks bar. You only need to do this once.

🛒 STS → RG Cart

Can't drag? Right-click the button → "Bookmark this link".

How to use

1

In the STS app, go to Supplies → Supplier Order. Check the items you need and enter amounts for each (OZT for wire/sheet, ft for chain, qty for everything else).

2

Click 🛒 Send to RG in the order panel footer. This copies your order to the clipboard and opens Rio Grande in a new tab.

3

Switch to the Rio Grande tab that just opened. Make sure you're logged in. Then click the STS → RG Cart bookmarklet from your bookmarks bar.

4

A progress overlay appears and adds all items to your cart automatically. When done, click View Cart to review before checkout.

Notes

• CTO items (wire, sheet) use OZT — the bookmarklet sends the exact weight you entered.

• Chain items (By the foot) use FOOT as the unit of measure.

• Per-pack items (jump rings, tools) use the qty stepper — bookmarklet sends that count.

• You must be logged into Rio Grande for the cart API to accept requests.

• If an item fails (400 error), RG may require it to be added manually — check the overlay log.
